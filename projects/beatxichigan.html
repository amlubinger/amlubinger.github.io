Description:

Beat ❌ichigan is a keyboard for iPhone®, iPad®, and iPod touch® which is (soon) available for download on the App Store®.


The keyboard is based on this open source project and replaces all m's with ❌ in the spirit of Beat Michigan week for The Ohio State University leading up to The Game. Development started 11/27/19.
