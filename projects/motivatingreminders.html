Description:

Motivating Reminders is an app for iPhone®, iPad®, and iPod touch® which is (soon) available for download on the App Store®.


Motivating Reminders is a reminders app developed in Swift with a focus on mental well-being. Development started in Fall 2018.


Motivating Reminders allows you to create an reminder for all of your life events. They can be repeated daily, weekly, monthly, yearly, or not at all. What sets Motivating Reminders apart from a regular reminders app however, is that it helps you balance your life. It will analyze your life based on categories and recommend improvements. It will also give you special messages based on key words in your reminders. It is designed to improve your daily mental well-being.
