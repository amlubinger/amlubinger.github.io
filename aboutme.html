I grew up in Beachwood, Ohio (a suburb of Cleveland). Currently, I am a fourth year Computer Science and Engineering student at The Ohio State University, graduating in the Spring. My program includes a specialization in software engineering. I am eager to continue learning and creating projects. I am currently working and learning as a Software Engineer Intern at MIM Software! This past summer, I worked as a Software Engineer Intern at JPMorgan Chase & Co! I am passionate about software development and technology as a whole.

I have been interested in technological advancements for as long as I can remember, but my interests really expanded when I took AP Computer Science in my sophomore year of high school. This class very quickly became my favorite and it was my first experience in programming. After such a positive experience, I went on to take a couple more programming classes: Honors Software Engineering and Honors Robotics. I also became a member of the Robotics team with a role in programming our robot. I joined our Science Olympiad team with an event leader role in my favorite event: Game On, in which a pair had to create a game using Scratch (a new type of programming to me). I also joined our Science and Engineering Fair Club. Through this club, I created an iPhone App and a Java program on my own and won awards. More recently, I have completed coursework and projects at The Ohio State University. I have participated in multiple Hack OHI/O hackathons and have worked on other projects in my free time. Along with my internship experiences, I even had the opportunity to develop a mobile app for West Virginia University, called WVU Involved. All of these experiences have impacted my knowledge and interests. My passion for software engineering has grown constantly since taking AP Computer Science and it is still continuing to grow with every project I complete.

My favorite part of programming is how it feels like solving different types of puzzles, all at the same time. From jigsaw to object search to crossword to brain teaser to cryptanalysis, I frequently notice elements of these puzzles in programming. I really enjoy working on these challenges.

I am extremely excited to continue learning at The Ohio State University, complete personal projects, and gain professional experience. After graduating with my degree, I hope to work as a software engineer. I am particularly interested in mobile application development. Eventually, I would like to create my own technology/software company.
