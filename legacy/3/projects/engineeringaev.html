Engineering AEV
Student Organization Tinder Hyland Software - Senior Project Writing Analytics Java Program Energy Switch App Personalized iPhone App Engineering Game Software Class Projects Personal Website
    Expand    

Created an autonomous electric vehicle in a team of 4. The small vehicle, propelled by small spinning blades, rode on a track attached to the ceiling. It had to stop and wait at a gate, pick up cargo with a magnet, proceed backwards to the gate, wait for it to open again, and return to the starting location. Development process included brainstorming, building, testing, adjusting, and programming.
